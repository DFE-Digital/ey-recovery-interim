Module 8: Supporting individual differences and needs

Identifying children’s developmental differences and specific needs is crucial to ensuring children can learn and develop. As an early years practitioner, you play a significant role in supporting inclusive environments where children can thrive and develop important life skills. Adapting your practice to meet the needs of all children is essential.

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

This module covers:

  • an understanding of how to identify developmental differences and needs
  • the factors affecting children’s development when they access the learning and curriculum on offer in the setting
