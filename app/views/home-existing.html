Child development training for early years providers

This free, online training provides an overview of child development and gives practical advice for supporting the development of children in your early years setting.

About the training

Each training module on this site covers an important area of child development and has been written by early years experts, working with the Department for Education and Ofsted.

Why should I take this training?

  • free training for childminders and nurseries
  • combination of theory and practical tips
  • opportunities to reflect on your practice
  • includes activity ideas you can do in your setting
  • links to additional resources
  • download a certificate of completion
